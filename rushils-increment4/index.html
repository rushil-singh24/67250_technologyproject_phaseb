Welcome to MonoMuse

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to exploring the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity through immersive exhibitions and interactive installations.

Buy Tickets

Through rotating collections, artist talks, and hands-on media experiences, MonoMuse invites visitors to discover how digital innovation is transforming the way we create, experience, and understand art. Whether you are exploring experimental projections or interactive sculpture, each visit is designed to spark curiosity and conversation.
